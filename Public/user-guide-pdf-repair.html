Batch Module

PDF Repair & Hardening

Clean, normalize and lock down risky documents before archiving or sharing.

🛠️

Normalize & OCR

Problem: Non-searchable scans or PDFs with unstable layers.

Solution: Renders every page into a fresh bitmap at controlled DPI (72-600) and reapplies a high-fidelity OCR layer.

  • Reconstructs broken internal PDF structures.
  • Choice of output quality vs file size.
  • Preserves original folder hierarchies.
🖼️

Flatten to Image-Only

Problem: Risk of "lifting" redactions or hidden metadata theft.

Solution: Strips all text layers and annotations, burning the visual content into a single-layer non-searchable document.

  • Guaranteed removal of all searchable content.
  • Maximum security for sensitive disclosure.
  • High-speed batch processing for thousands of docs.
📄

Office PDF Converter

Problem: Mixed Word/Excel trees that need audit-ready PDF versions.

Solution: Batch converts entire directory trees of .doc, .docx, .xls, .xlsx into professional PDF format.

  • Zero disruption to your folder tree architecture.
  • Handles password-protected files (if provided).
  • High reliability for forensic evidence bundles.
✒️

Batch Watermark

Problem: Need to brand thousands of documents for case files.

Solution: Overlays configurable text watermarks across batches while mirroring the input structure.

  • Custom text (e.g., "STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL").
  • Adjustable opacity and positioning.
  • Super-fast processing on local hardware.
